# Skill: Medical Chart Pro (@vis/medical-chart-pro)

## Purpose
Specialized visualization for pediatric developmental tracking.

## Capabilities
- **Advanced Radar Chart**: 5-Axis (Cognition, Sensory, Motor, Behavior, Social).
- **Comparative Line Plot**: Baseline (100% typical child) vs. Current progress.
- **Alert Logic**:
  - Green: +5% improvement.
  - Yellow: Stagnation (>4 weeks).
  - Red: Regression.

## Contextual Knowledge
- Child growth percentiles for developmental age.
- Normalizes "100%" to age-appropriate motor/social benchmarks.
- Interactive drill-down for each "Skill Sector".
